About Me

Bhavdiya Pandya is a passionate Computer Science and Engineering student with a strong foundation in full-stack development, algorithms, and system design. Proficient in JavaScript, React, Node.js, Vue, Angular, and Next.js, he has hands-on experience in UI/UX design, API development, and cloud computing. He is skilled in data structures, dynamic programming, and greedy algorithms, with a strong problem-solving mindset. Bhavdiya has demonstrated leadership through his role as a lead organizer of programming events and workshops, along with conducting a Python workshop. He has gained industry exposure through internships at Hindustan Zinc Pvt Ltd and in Java Spring development, contributing to dynamic site development and IT infrastructure management. A dedicated hackathon participant, he secured 2nd place in an intercollege hackathon and competed in IIT Roorkee’s Cognizance event. His extracurricular interests include sketching, doodling, and playing table tennis at a state level. He is actively looking to grow in software engineering, aiming for top tech roles at Google or Microsoft, and continuously enhancing his technical and problem-solving skills
